Kobe Bryant Fan Club is a community for people who respect, appreciate, support and love Kobe Bryant. We all, in some way, inspired by the man Kobe Bryant. The greatest player in NBA history

Kobe is a legend, an icon of basketball and a perfect demonstration of dedication, determination and discipline. Over his twenty years with Los Angeles Lakers, he won five NBA Championships, 18-time All-Star, 15-time member of the All-NBA Team, 2008 NBA Most Valuable Player (MVP) and two-time NBA Finals MVP.
